How I Prepared for GATE CH

Preparing for GATE Chemical Engineering requires proper planning and consistency. I started my preparation by understanding the complete syllabus and dividing it into manageable subjects.

My strategy was:

  • Build strong fundamentals in Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, and Heat Transfer.
  • Solve previous year GATE questions regularly.
  • Take mock tests to improve speed and accuracy.
  • Revise short notes every week.

Consistency is the key. Even studying 4–5 focused hours daily can create a big difference.

Remember, GATE is not about studying everything — it is about mastering important concepts.
